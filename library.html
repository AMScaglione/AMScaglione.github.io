Welcome to the Library of Babel
Contained within the walls of this library designed by Jorge Luis Borges is every possible 410 page book containing every possible combination of 3200 letter pages. Within these pages is every novel ever written, and every novel that has yet to be. Every song, every scientific discovery known and unknown, every name of every person who will ever live.

All inside this archive .

The collection is divided into hexagonal rooms. Each room has four walls, each wall has five shelves, each shelf has 32 books, and each book has 411 pages. Every page can be accessed at any time provided you know it's address. Feel free to browse at your discretion, or should you so choose you may even search for specific passages within the texts. Should you require help simply search for "help", and if you want to know more about the workings of this portal, search for "technical documentation". Enjoy your stay.
